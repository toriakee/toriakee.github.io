How SLABS Started

SLABS was born as a product of furlough during the Covid-19 pandemic, when I turned to making ethical chocolate to keep me sane.

Having always been obsessed with (addicted to) dark chocolate, my growing concern for the trading ethics surrounding cocoa farming pushed me to source my own ingredients, start from scratch, and create my own chocolate.

Chocolate Making

Chocolatiers, along with a large volume of 'homemade', 'small batch' chocolate producers you'll find typically buy ready-made chocolate and just add flavouring, repackage it and sell on for a profit.

Not to be confused with a 'chocolatier', a 'chocolate maker' is involved with sourcing ingredients, deciding recipes, ratios of cocoa solids in each bar and the manufacturing process.

SLABS is passionate about using only the finest, ethically sourced ingredients. Handcrafting each individual slab of chocolate puts SLABS at the heart of chocolate making.
