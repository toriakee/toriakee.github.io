Custom Chocolate

The chocolate is handmade from raw ingredients, which gives you the freedom to request any percentage of cocoa solids you'd like in your bar, tailored to your favourite tastes. Alternatively, just choose SLABS "Original" or "Silk" recipes, and add fruit and/or nuts (or don't)! It's totally up to you and your tastebuds:

  • Any dark percentage over 60%
  • Organic, ethically sourced cashew nut pieces
  • Organic, ethically sourced raisin pieces

    • Details

    All chocolate is created from 3 simple ingredients: Criollo cacao mass, raw cacao butter, and raw cane sugar.
    All ethically sourced single-origin cacao from small farms in Peru who's workers are paid properly. Chocolate that tastes good, and does good!

  • Organic
  • Vegan
  • Dairy free
  • Gluten free
  • Low sugar
  • No vegetable oils, palm oils or cheap fats which may be found in mainstream chocolate
  • Free from absolutely anything artificial
